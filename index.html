FAQs

What is Covid-19?

Covid 19 is a disease caused by SARS-CoV-2 virus.Most people who fall sick with coivd 19 will experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment. However, some will be seriously ill and require medical attention.

How does the virus spread?

Covid 19 spreads when an infected person breathes out droplets and very small particles that contain the virus.These droplets and particles can be breathed in by other people.

Should i use soap and water or hand sanitiser to protect myself?

Handwashing is one of the best ways to protect yourself but if it is not available use a hand sanitiser with atleast 60% alcohol.

Can my pets or other animals get covid?

As yet there is no evidence to prove that pet animals can spread covid 19 but be careful and avoid physical contact if you are infected.

When should I seek emergency care if I have Covid-19?

If someone has signs like persistent pain in the chest, trouble breathing, inability to stay awake seek emergency care immediately.
